Why am I applying to the Johnson Cornell Tech MBA?

7 reasons why I will be thrilled to be part of Cornell Tech MBA

Reason N-1

Despite the fact that Latin-America is growing rapidly in terms of innovation and startups I truly believe that I need to get exposed to the best entrepreneurial ecosystems in the world so I started looking for a top MBA program with a strong orientation to technology until I found in Cornell Tech MBA the perfect match

Reason N-2

I am looking to expand my technological knowlegde and this program will offer me the chance to accomplish that goal.

Reason N-3

The structure of the Cornell Tech MBA is exactly what I was looking for in a post-graduate program (Business + Tech)

Reason N-4

I agree with the importance of recalling the core courses of business but it is even more important for a tech-entrepreneur to be actively involve helping to and learning from the best entrepreneurs in the world (Product Studio)

Reason N-5

My ultimate goal is to develop a new and state-of-the-art startup with the help of my tech-savvy classmates (Startup Studio)

Reason N-6

I am also excited about the possibility to share thoughts and opinions with the top-notch members of the faculty.

Reason N-7

Living in a city like New York is a huge plus due to the important events that take place in the City
