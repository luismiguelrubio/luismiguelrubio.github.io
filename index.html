Why am I applying to the Johnson Cornell Tech MBA?

7 reasons why I will be thrilled to be part of Cornell Tech MBA

Reason N-1

Despite the fact that Latin-America is growing rapidly in terms of innovation and startups, I truly believe that I need to get exposed to the best entrepreneurial ecosystems in the world, so I started looking for a top MBA program strongly oriented toward technology when I found in Cornell Tech MBA the perfect match.

Reason N-2

I want to acquire more tech-related knowledge and interact with tech-savy classmates.

Reason N-3

The structure of the Cornell Tech MBA is exactly what I was looking for in a graduate program (Business + Tech).

Reason N-4

I agree with the importance of reinforcing the material of the core courses of business but consider it even more important for a tech-entrepreneur to be actively involved in working with and learning from the best entrepreneurs in the world (Product Studio).

Reason N-5

My ultimate goal is to develop a new and state-of-the-art startup with the help of tech-savvy classmates (Startup Studio). Marketplaces such as Uber and Airbnb, and E-commerce websites such as Etsy are my favorites.

Reason N-6

I am also excited about the possibility of sharing thoughts and opinions with the experienced and knowledgeable members of the faculty.

Reason N-7

Living in a city like New York is a huge plus due to the important events that take place in the City.

Technical skills and professional experience


I have been involved in the Latin-American entrepreneurial Ecosystem since 2011. I have lived in Peru, Argentina and Mexico and I have strong relationships with the most important venture capitalists and the most successful entrepreneurs in Latin-America . The extent of my sphere of professional contacts is partly thanks to the exposure I get for being part of the 500 Startups Community . I know how hard the path to start a company with ten people and ended up with more than 200 people is. I have started companies from scratch, as an entrepreneur and as an intrapreneur , hiring and firing people over the uneasy course of finding the right organizational structure. Having worked with developers for a long time, I began learning to code . I found that a working knowledge of their domain makes working with developers much easier.

Short and long-term career goals
Powered by Cornell Tech MBA :)

I will find the right partner during the MBA program, and together we will select the most promising idea taking into consideration our respective skills and the target market.
